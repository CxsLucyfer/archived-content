Notes for Venezuela

  • Last updated June 13, 2012.
  • While Venezuela has its own currency, the Firefox Marketplace only accepts US$.
  • There is no tax calculation for Venezuela.
  • We do not support operator billing at this time in Venezuela.
  • The payout calculations have VAT deducted: (Price paid/(1+VAT)) x 0.70 for operator billing and credit cards.
