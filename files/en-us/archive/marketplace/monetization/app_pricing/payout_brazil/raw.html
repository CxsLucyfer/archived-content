Notes for Brazil

  • Last updated June 13, 2012.
  • R$ is the symbol for Brazilian reals.
  • Exchange rate used in calculation for pricing: R$2.0354 per US$1.00.
  • Brazil pricing is tax-exclusive. Users may have to pay Brazilian taxes on top of the price.
  • Payout calculation: price paid x 0.70 for both credit cards and operator billing.
