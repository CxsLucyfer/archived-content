Notes for Colombia

  • Last updated September 07, 2013.
  • COP$ is the symbol for Colombian pesos.
  • Exchange rate used in the pricing calculation: COP$1949.32 per US$1.00.
  • Colombia pricing is tax inclusive. It includes VAT of 16%.
  • The price paid: (USD price x COP$1949.32) x 1.16.
  • The payouts have VAT deducted: (price paid/(1+VAT)) x 0.70 for operator billing, (price paid x 0.70) for credit cards.
  • The minimum credit card payment is US$0.99 and the maximum credit card payment is US$30.00.
