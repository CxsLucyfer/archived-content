Notes for Spain

  • Last updated June 13, 2012.
  • € is the symbol for euros in Spain.
  • Exchange rate used in pricing calculation: €.7679 per US$1.00.
  • Spain pricing is tax-inclusive. It includes VAT of 21%.
  • The payout calculations have VAT deducted: (Price paid/(1+VAT)) x 0.70 for operator billing and credit cards.
