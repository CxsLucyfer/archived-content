Scenario

Frank, a MDC contributor, visits MDC regularly to maintain and edit pages.

Walkthrough

  • Frank goes to the MDC home page and clicks "Login" on the navigation bar to login to the MDC Wiki.
  • Frank then clicks "Watchlist" on the navigation bar to see if there have been any changes to the list of pages that he owns. There aren't any today.
  • Frank goes back to the MDC home page and clicks on "See more changes to articles" under "Recent Changes" to get a full change history from the last few days. He notices an article with a change, and clicks on the title to be brought to the content page for the article.
  • On that page, Frank notices a grammatical error, and clicks "Edit" in the page tools to edit the page.
