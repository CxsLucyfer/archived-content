The Orange Klif is a 3G Smartphone, powered by Firefox OS 2.0, with a 2 megapixel camera. It is available in a number of African countries.

  • Official Website of Orange Klif

Countries available

  • Cameroon
  • Madagascar
  • Mauritius
  • Senegal
  • Tunisia
  • Ivory Coast
  • Kenya

 

Device specifications

You can find more of the device specifications listed on the Features and Specs Page.

  • CPU: Mediatek Dual core 1GHz CPU, MT6572M
  • Dimensions:112.2 x 62 x 11.9 mm
  • Display: 3.5" 320 x 480 pixels TFT capacitive touch
  • Camera: 2.0 MP rear, LED flash
  • Battery: Li-Ion 1300 mAh battery
  • Resolution: 320 x 480 (~165 ppi pixel density)
  • Internal memory:512 MB ROM, 256 MB RAM
  • External memory: microSD (32GB max.)
  • Weight: Approx. 98 g
  • GPS: GPS / A-GPS
  • Networks: GSM / HSPA
  • Wireless: Bluetooth v3.0 Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n
  • Ports: Micro USB 2.0 high speed 3.5 mm stereo headset jack
  • Radio: Stereo FM

Colors Available

  • Black
  • White
