The Alcatel One Touch Fire E smartphone is powered by Firefox OS; it has a dual core 1.2 GHz processor, high screen resolution and a 5 MP Rear Camera + VGA front camera; it is available in a number of different parts of the world.

Get a device

The Alcatel One Touch Fire E is available through standard retail channels.

Countries Available

  • Czech Republic
  • Germany
  • Hungary
  • Poland
  • Russia

Operators

  • Congstar
  • MegaFon
  • O2
  • T-Mobile

 

Updating & upgrading the software

The device's system image is locked, so you can only update the software when the network operator releases an OTA update.

Device specifications

You can find more of the device specifications listed on Alcatel's Website under Specification tab

  • CPU:Dual Core 1.2 GHz CPU
  • Camera: 5 MP, 2592 х 1944 pixels, autofocus, LED flash , VGA Front Camera
  • Battery: 1700 mAh
  • Display: 4.5” qHD 960 x 540 pixels, IPS 16M color display
  • Resolution: 540x960 pixels
  • Internal memory: 4GB emmc ROM / 512MB RAM
  • External memory: MicroSD Card: up to 32GB
  • Size: 129 x 63.5 x 8.2 mm
  • Weight: Approx. 103g
  • SIM: Micro SIM card, EAP SIM

Colors Available
