The Spice Fire One MI FX1 is a consumer Firefox OS smart phone, based on Tarako Firefox OS, which is released in India. It was released by Spice Smartphones in August 2014.

Get a device

The phone is discontinued.

Countries available

India

Updating & upgrading the software

The device's system image is locked, so you can only update the software when the network operator releases an OTA update. The device system image is based on Firefox OS 1.3T.

Device specifications

You can find more of the device specifications listed on our Phone and device specs page.

  • CPU: 1GHz
  • Camera:  1.3 megapixel back + VGA front camera
  • Battery: 1400 mAh
  • Display: 8.89 HVGA
  • Resolution: 320 x 480
  • Internal memory: 256MB ROM, 128MB RAM   
  • External memory: microSD (4GB max.)
  • Size: 115.6x62x11.2mm
  • Weight: Approx. 104g (with battery)

Colors Available

  • Black
  • White
