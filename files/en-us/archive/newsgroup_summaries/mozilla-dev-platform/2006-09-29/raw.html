Discussions

  • Xulrunner fails without feedback on OSX Intel 10.4.7
    • Will Morton is trying to port a XUL application to OSX. When he runs xulrunner from the command line, it fails silently without any errors. This happens with all his xulrunner ports, with xulrunner 1.8.0.1, 1.8.0.4 and nightly 1.8. Benjamin Smedberg was able to point out one possible flaw. Instead of using /path/to/xulrunner-bin.ini (which Will was doing) applications on a mac must be in an application bundle (Foo.app). Then use xulrunner -install-app to "unpack" the XUL app into the Mac structure.
