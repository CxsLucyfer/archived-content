This section provides the various policies and guidelines for Firefox Marketplace.
The following policies and guidelines are available for Firefox Marketplace:
 

Policies

Privacy Policies
If your app uses personal data about a user, it must have a privacy policy.

Guidelines

Privacy policy
These guidelines cover the information you need to include in your app's privacy policy, when it uses a user's personal data.
Screenshot guidelines
These guidelines provide information on the best approaches to take for screenshots of your app, which you include in its Marketplace listing.
App testing and troubleshooting
These guidelines provide information on setting up a test environment, testing your app and troubleshooting issues you may find.
