Before you can submit an app to the Firefox Marketplace you need a developer account, to have signed into that account, and accepted the developer agreement.

Open the Developer Hub

  1. In a Web browser, visit the Firefox Marketplace.
  2. At the bottom of the Firefox Marketplace page, click (1) Developer Hub.

Start the app submission process

  1. On the Developer Hub home page, click (1) Submit your app to the Marketplace.
  2. The Please sign in dialog displays, click (1) Sign in / Sign up.
  3. The next step will depend on whether you have a Mozilla Persona:
    • if you don't a Persona, you'll be promoted to create one:
      You can find out more about Persona here.
      1. Enter your email address and click Next.
      2. Enter a new password and enter a confirmation, click Done.
      3. An email will be sent to the address you provided. This email contains a confirmation link under Confirm your account now, click this link.
      4. In the page displayed (which will be the same as shown in step 2 above) click Sign in / Sign up
    • If you've a Persona, you'll be prompted to confirm the email address you want to use to access your developer account

      Click sign in
  4. You'll now see the Developer Agreement.
    1. review the Developer Agreement
    2. agree to being sent App Developer news and surveys (if you wish)
    3. click Agree and Continue

You can now proceed to the next step: Loading your app
