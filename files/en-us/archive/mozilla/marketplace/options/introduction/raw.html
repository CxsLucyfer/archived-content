The Firefox Marketplace provides a channel through which you can publish your apps and make them easily discoverable by users of Firefox OS. However, it is not the only option you have for making your apps available. This section will help you to get some basic ideas about the formats in which you can deliver your apps and  various options for publishing apps.

Mobile-optimized websites
The Firefox Marketplace has the facility to list Mobile-optimized websites (MOW) — simply websites that work well across mobile devices and platforms — right next to apps.
