About

  • Status: In Development
  • Instantiation: jetpack.future.import('slideBar')
  • Last Update: v0.6
  • Summary: slideBar is a reinvention of the old sidebar feature of browsers. They allow quick access to a wide range of both temporary and permanent information at the side of your browser window. When a slideBar feature is selected, its contents will be revealed from behind the current web page.

Creating a SlideBar and adding options

To implement a new slideBar within your jetpack code, use the method jetpack.slideBar.append(options)
