Represents the menu bar (positioned beneath the title bar of a window) from which menus are selected by the user.

Interfaces

  • nsIAccessNode
  • nsIAccessible

Events

  • EVENT_MENU_START - fired when underlying ROLE_MENUITEM is focused.

Mapped to

  • AT-SPI: ROLE_MENU_BAR
  • ATK: ATK_ROLE_MENU_BAR
  • UA: NSAccessibilityMenuBarRole
  • MSAA: ROLE_SYSTEM_MENUBAR

Used by

  • ARIA: role="menubar"
  • XUL: <menubar/>
