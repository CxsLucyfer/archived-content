Description

A glass pane. A pane that is guaranteed to be painted on top of all panes beneath it. Also refer to ROLE_ROOT_PANE.

Mapped to

  • AT-SPI: ROLE_GLASS_PANE
  • ATK: ATK_ROLE_GLASS_PANE
  • UA: NSAccessibilityGroupRole
  • MSAA : ROLE_SYSTEM_APPLICATION
  • IA2: IA2_ROLE_GLASS_PANE
