Represents the window frame, which contains child objects such as a title bar, client, and other objects contained in a window.

Mapped to

  • AT-SPI: ROLE_WINDOW
  • ATK: ATK_ROLE_WINDOW
  • UA: NSAccessibilityWindowRole
  • MSAA/IA2: ROLE_SYSTEM_WINDOW

Used by

The role is supported automatically by Microsoft Windows.
