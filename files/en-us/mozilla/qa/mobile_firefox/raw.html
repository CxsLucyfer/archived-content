Firefox for Android is the mobile version of Firefox with a native Android look and feel. It's built using the same Gecko platform as Firefox on desktop. Phones and tablets are supported on Android. To learn more about us, visit our home page.

What Do We Aim For?

  • Get the best quality of your favorite open-source mobile browser
  • Build a dedicated community of testers for Mobile Firefox
  • Educate people in testing principles and methodologies, regarding mobile browsing
